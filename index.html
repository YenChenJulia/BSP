Frequently asked general questions

1. What is an Online Personal Loan?

It's an unsecured loan offered through the Easy App by Bank SinoPac, following the bank's policies and Vietnamese banking regulations.

2. What documents do I need for the loan application?

Valid ID, active mobile phone number, and financial documents (e.g., salary statements from the last 3 months, labor contract, etc.).

3. What is the maximum loan amount I can apply for?

You can borrow up to 500 million VND per loan.

4. Do I need a guarantor or collateral?

No, this is an unsecured loan, so no guarantor or collateral is required.

5. How is the monthly repayment calculated, and how do I pay?

Equal monthly installments based on the reducing balance. Payments are automatically deducted from your demand account each month.

6. What is the maximum loan duration?

You can select a loan tenor of up to 48 months.

7. What happens if I miss a payment?

Late payments are reported to the Credit Information Center (CIC) and may incur additional interest based on the credit agreement.

8. How will the bank remind me to make repayments?

SMS reminders will be sent before your repayment is due. Ensure you have enough funds in your SinoPac account before the due date.

9. When will I receive the loan approval notification?

Bank SinoPac will send an SMS immediately after your loan is approved.

10. How long will it take to receive the loan?

The loan amount will be credited to your account within 1-2 working days after approval.

11. Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can repay your loan early, but there may be fees based on your credit agreement.

12. What happens after I finish paying off the loan?

Your loan account will be closed, and a final statement will be issued.

13. Are there any penalties for early or missed payments?

Late or missed payments may incur additional fees. Early repayment fees depend on your loan agreement.

14. Can I apply for a second loan while repaying my first loan?

Your eligibility for a second loan depends on your current credit standing and repayment history.

15. Can I change my monthly installment date?

Contact the bank for support. Changes are possible under certain conditions.

16. Can I apply for this loan if I have loans with other banks?

Yes, but your application will be assessed based on your credit history and debt levels.

17. How will I receive the loan contract?

Bank SinoPac will send the contract to your registered email. The file will be encrypted with a password (your ID number).

Loan Application

  • 1 Download and Register

    Download the Easy App, register with personal information, and verify your identity.

  • 2 Complete Account Setup

    Review information, and set password.

  • 3 Access Loan Application

    Login to the Easy app, and select "Online Loan Application".

  • 4 Review Loan Details

    Check interest rate, loan amount, and tenor.

  • 5 Provide Loan Details

    Choose the amount, and tenor, verify identity, and agree to terms.

  • 6 Submit Application

    Provide additional info and documents, and submit an application.

Loan Disbursement

  • 1 Receive Approval Notification

    Get an SMS with a contract link.

  • 2 Register for Digital Certificate

    Register a CA, verify your ID, and complete a video.

  • 3 Sign Loan Contract

    Log in to the Easy app, review the contract, and sign with your PIN.

  • 4 Loan Disbursement

    Receive confirmation of disbursement via registered SMS an email.
